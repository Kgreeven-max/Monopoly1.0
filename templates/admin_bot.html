Bot Strategies

Conservative Strategy
Conservative bots focus on maintaining cash reserves and making safe investments. They prefer to avoid risky trades and only develop properties when they have a strong cash position.
Aggressive Strategy
Aggressive bots prioritize property acquisition and development, even at the risk of cash shortages. They actively pursue trades and auctions to complete property sets.
Strategic Strategy
Strategic bots take a balanced approach, focusing on completing property sets and making calculated investments based on return-on-investment analysis.
Opportunistic Strategy
Opportunistic bots adapt their strategy based on the game state and other players' behaviors. They look for bargains and special opportunities, particularly during economic downturns.
